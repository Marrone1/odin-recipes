Fish Tacos!

Description

You don't have to leave your house to enjoy a perfect fish tacos. This recipe will be your go to when having friends over or

just want to have a casual date in your home. Come check out how simple it is to make.



Ingredients

  • Cod, tilapia or sea bass fillets;
  • Homemade white sauce (with yogurt, mayonnaise, lime juice, cayenne pepper and seasoning);
  • Fresh cabbage;
  • Tortillas or tacos.

Preparation

  1. Make the beer batter.
  2. Make the white sauce.
  3. Dust the fish in flour, then dip in the batter.
  4. Fry the fish until golden brown.
  5. Serve on tortillas with the sauce and cabbage.
